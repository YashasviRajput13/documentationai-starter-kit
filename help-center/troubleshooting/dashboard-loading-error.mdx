---
title: Dashboard Not Loading
description: Fix common dashboard loading problems.
---

## Symptoms

- Blank page after login
- Spinning loader does not stop

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Internet Connection">
    ````bash
    ping dashboard.neurogrid.city
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear Browser Cache">
    Try reloading after clearing your cache/settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try Incognito Mode">
    Browser add-ons can block the dashboard; incognito disables them.
  </Step>
</Steps>
