## Logging In

You need a Neuro Grid account to access the dashboard.

1. Go to `https://dashboard.neurogrid.city`
2. Click **Login** in the top right
3. Enter your organizational email
4. Enter your password and click **Sign In**

## Access Problems?

- Use **Forgot Password** to reset
- Still no access? Contact your admin
